# Bug Report UE-001: Camera Clipping

**Summary:** Camera clips through geometry near map boundaries

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Launch Third Person Template
2. Navigate to corner of map
3. Move camera close to wall

**Expected Result:** Camera should maintain proper distance from walls

**Actual Result:** Camera passes through wall geometry

**Severity:** Medium
**Priority:** Low
**Environment:** UE 5.x, Windows 11
